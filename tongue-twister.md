# Important Pronunciation Tricks

## TH

Tongue between teeth

Examples:

* think
* three
* thank

---

## V

Upper teeth touch lower lip

Examples:

* very
* voice
* victory

---

## W

Round lips

Examples:

* well
* welcome
* wonder

---

## R

Tongue slightly curled

Examples:

* red
* rural
* really
