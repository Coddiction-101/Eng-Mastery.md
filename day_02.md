# Subject & “To Be” Verbs

## 1. Subject
The subject is the person or thing we talk about in a sentence.

### Examples:
- I am happy.
- She is smart.
- They are students.

Here:
- I
- She
- They

= Subjects

---

# 2. Subject Pronouns

These are common subject pronouns used in English:

| Pronoun | Meaning |
|----------|----------|
| I | myself |
| You | another person |
| He | a male person |
| She | a female person |
| It | a thing / animal |
| We | group including me |
| They | people / things |

---

# 3. “To Be” Verbs

The basic “to be” verbs are:

- am
- is
- are

These verbs change according to the subject.

---

# Sentence Formula

Subject + To Be + Information

### Examples:
- I am Aryan.
- He is tired.
- We are friends.

---

# Verb Changes with Subject

| Subject | Verb |
|----------|------|
| I | am |
| He / She / It | is |
| You / We / They | are |

---

# Quick Examples

- I am learning English.
- She is my friend.
- He is busy.
- We are students.
- They are happy.

---

# Easy Trick

- “am” → only with I
- “is” → one person or thing
- “are” → more than one person or thing
