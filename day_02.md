# 1. Subject
The subject is the person or thing we talk about in a sentence.

### Examples:
- I am happy.
- She is smart.
- They are students.

Here:
- I
- She
- They

= Subjects

---

# 2. Subject Pronouns

These are common subject pronouns used in English:

| Pronoun | Meaning |
|----------|----------|
| I | myself |
| You | another person |
| He | a male person |
| She | a female person |
| It | a thing / animal |
| We | group including me |
| They | people / things |

---

# 3. “To Be” Verbs

The basic “to be” verbs are:

- am
- is
- are

These verbs change according to the subject.

---

# Sentence Formula

Subject + To Be + Information

### Examples:
- I am Aryan.
- He is tired.
- We are friends.

---

# Verb Changes with Subject

| Subject | Verb |
|----------|------|
| I | am |
| He / She / It | is |
| You / We / They | are |

---

# Quick Examples

- I am learning English.
- She is my friend.
- He is busy.
- We are students.
- They are happy.

---

# Easy Trick

- “am” → only with I
- “is” → one person or thing
- “are” → more than one person or thing

---

# Practice Lines

- I am ready.
- You are smart.
- She is kind.
- We are learning.

# 1. Asking Age

### Common Questions

- How old are you?
- What is your age? *(formal)*

---

# 2. Saying Your Age

### Sentence Formula

Subject + am/is/are + age

### Examples

- I am 18 years old.
- She is 20 years old.
- They are 25 years old.

---

# 3. Natural English

People usually say:

- I am 18.
- He is 21.
- She is 19.

“years old” is optional in casual English.

---

# 4. Important Rule

Use:
- am → with I
- is → with He / She / It
- are → with You / We / They

---

# 5. Examples

| Subject | Sentence |
|----------|-----------|
| I | I am 18 years old. |
| You | You are 20 years old. |
| He | He is 25 years old. |
| She | She is 19 years old. |
| We | We are 18 years old. |
| They | They are 30 years old. |

---

# 6. Common Mistakes

❌ I have 18 years.  
✅ I am 18 years old.

---

❌ My age is 18 years old.  
✅ I am 18 years old.

---

# 7. Practice Lines

- I am 18 years old.
- How old are you?
- She is younger than me.
- He is 21 years old.
- We are the same age.
